Honouring 2025 Academic Achievement at High Achievers' Assembly

On Thursday 18 September, our Year 11 students officially transitioned into our leading year group of 2026.

This is a significant milestone in the school life of our students and the sanctity of the occasion is celebrated with the Black Tie Liturgy.

During the Liturgy, we reflected on the letter St Paul wrote to the Corinthians about LOVE. Dr Lavorato broke down the meaning of agape love, explaining that as leaders of the College, our Year 12 students are called to think before they act and refrain from selfish motivation, humbly considering others before themselves. In short, the Class of 2026 is challenged to 'love' all the boys at St Patrick's and to put their needs above their own.

 

After the blessing of the ties, each member of the Class of 2026 received their ties and Year 12 Badges before taking the traditional walk through the Via Sacra.

We strive to animate our delivery of learning, teaching and wellbeing, as one which is innovative and creative, underpinned by contemporary and relevant pedagogy.
