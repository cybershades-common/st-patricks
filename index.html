Let your light shine.

An independent Catholic day school for boys from Years 5 to 12 in the Edmund Rice tradition educating spirited men of faith and purpose.

Inspired by the gospel and Edmund Rice, St Patrick's College liberates the whole person and educates for resilient, spirited and inclusive men who are challenged to create a better world for all.

St Patrick's College continues to be a 'lighthouse' school for boys' education in Australia. As a College, we wish to stand tall and strong, illuminating our community from within and shining a light over our students to guide them to a safe harbour.

As reflected in our motto, Luceat Lux Vestra, we are committed to nurturing and challenging each boy to let his light shine. Our graduates will be men of integrity and wisdom who are called to share their light with others.

Our Strategic
Future and Growth

As part of our commitment to excellence and growth, we are investing in the Scientia Building, a state-of-the-art centre for science, hospitality and learning.

It will support cross-curricular education and foster a culture of innovation. We continue to shape a future where learning and community flourish.

Academics
and Learning

At SPC, learning is more than grades: it's about growth. We invest in quality teaching, a supportive wellbeing framework and learning pathways tailored to each student's strengths. From Years 5 through 12, we challenge our students to pursue academic excellence while developing character and leadership.

CO-CURRICULAR

Excellence on the Field and in the Pool

Our students compete at local, state and national levels across more than 20 sports. Professional coaching and a culture of teamwork develop athletes of discipline and resilience.

Music, Drama and Creative Expression Across the College

Students take part in ensembles, bands, drama groups and productions that develop skill, discipline and joy in performance.

Forming Young Men of Conscience and Action

Inspired by Edmund Rice, our students engage with social justice through service learning, advocacy campaigns and community partnerships.

Celebrating Diversity and Shared Heritage

Through cultural events, language programs and community celebrations, students develop a deep appreciation of the diverse world they will one day lead.

Discover Your Passion Beyond the Classroom

From robotics to debating, chess to coding — our clubs give every student a place to pursue interests, build friendships and develop leadership.

Learning That Extends Beyond Our Gates

From local excursions and outdoor education camps to international immersion tours, students broaden their horizons and deepen their understanding of the world.

Nearly a century of formative education
